Features

Built for how dispensaries actually operate

Not a chatbot. Not a quiz. A real tool your team will actually use during a transaction.

01 — Recommendation Engine
Tap an effect. Get three products.
Customer says they want to relax. Budtender taps Relax. Cannascope shows the top 3 products from your live inventory — scored by terpene profile and effect match.
  • Six effect categories: Sleep, Relax, Focus, Social, Relief, Balanced
  • Results scored using real terpene data — not just indica/sativa
  • Always pulls from your live, in-stock inventory
02 — Manager Notes
Tell your team what to push — without saying a word.
Managers attach notes directly to products: "Overstock — push first," "High margin," "Brand promo this week." Those notes appear on the recommendation card, right below the talking point.
  • Notes surface automatically during recommendations
  • Directs budtender attention without a daily briefing
  • Updated in real time from the Admin panel
03 — Talking Points
Every product has a script. Nobody has to wing it.
Cannascope generates a natural-language talking point for every product — grounded in terpenes, potency, and format. New budtenders sound experienced from Day 1.
  • Auto-generated from product data on import
  • Editable in the Admin panel
  • Consistent across the whole floor — no conflicting recommendations
04 — Upsell Prompts
Basket size grows automatically.
After showing the main recommendations, Cannascope surfaces a contextual upsell suggestion — tied to the effect category. No extra training. No pressure. Just a prompt.
  • Effect-specific upsell logic (relax flower → gummies for longer effect)
  • Shown at the bottom of every recommendation screen
  • Designed to feel like helpful advice, not a hard sell
