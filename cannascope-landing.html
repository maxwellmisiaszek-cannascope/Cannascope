The Problem

The sales floor is broken
at most dispensaries.

Not because of bad staff. Because there's no system. And without a system, every transaction depends on whoever's working that shift.

01
New staff takes months to get good
It takes 60–90 days for a new hire to know the menu well enough to recommend confidently. Until then, they under-sell, over-apologize, and default to whatever the customer already knows.
02
Inventory moves at random
Managers have no reliable way to direct staff toward overstock or high-margin items. Products sit while customers walk out with whatever was recommended first — not what you need to move.
03
Upsells never happen
Budtenders are focused on getting through the transaction. Without a systematic prompt, basket size stays flat. There's no reason to suggest an add-on — so nobody does.
